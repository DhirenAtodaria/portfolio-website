The
Drop

The Drop is an intuitive and easy to use web application. It's sole purpose is to notify/update users on the current music releases and includes features such as Spotify API integration and a full user/artist following feature. It was built during the _nology course with a team of 15 members, The primary technologies used are React and Firebase. I was responsible for developing a full following feature and integrating it to the various parts of the database/app.

Oznom

Oznom is a complete money handling app. It's designed to allow a user to enter their daily, monthly and yearly spends in a nice intuitive UI experience, in-addition, it's fully authenticated with error messages and each account can enter their own spendings. However, above all else, the main feature is the collation of all the information and presenting it back to the user in a responsive line graph. The technologies used are React, d3.js, Semantic UI and Firebase

The Tech_nology story.

My journey begins when I was at university. You see that picture on the right? That's it. I went to the University of Warwick to study Maths and Stats. I had an amazing time and learnt a lot, got to meet many new people. But one thing, that stuck out throughout those years, was I had absolutely no idea what kind of career I wanted to do. I thought hard and long but nothing seemed appealing at the time. So I did what any other confused young adult would do. Go and live in China.

Upon returning, after having experienced huge personal development/growth in China, I did an internship with BT, where I got to learn more about the world of tech and what it contains. It was around this time I came across _nology. It is a 12-week course designed to immerse and provide you with all you need to begin an exciting career, as a new front-end developer. Having realised coding was what I wanted to do with a career, I proceeded to join _nology.

I began the course late January. It began with the basics: HTML/CSS/JS. I completed a lots of challenges, including creating games and completing other hard problems. Upon learning these for a few weeks, we trasnitioned onto React. I learned about the React ecosystem, and the possibilities of what could be created using React. In-addition, we undertook a client project called 'The Drop'. A fully featured MVP for a client who will proceed to gain investment using it. The app was created in 5 weeks and contains many different features such as Spotify Integration, a full following feature and more. It was also around this time I finally completed a final project called Oznom, a money budgeting app. The future is exciting, and I can't wait for what comes after _nology.
